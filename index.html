Our Practices and

Legal Institutions Visits

At the undergraduate level, our college offers a bachelor's degree in law that provides a strong foundation in legal theory and practice. The program covers a wide range of legal subjects, including contract law, criminal law, constitutional law, and tort law, among others. In addition to traditional coursework, students have the opportunity to participate in mock trials, legal clinics, and internships to gain hands-on experience in the field.

At the postgraduate level, we offer a variety of specialized master's and doctoral programs in areas such as corporate law, international law, and environmental law, to name a few. These programs are designed to provide advanced training and research opportunities for students who want to pursue careers as legal scholars, practitioners, or policy makers.

In addition to our formal degree programs, we also offer a number of continuing education courses for professionals who want to stay up-to-date on the latest developments in the legal field. These courses cover a range of topics, including legal ethics, new legislation, and emerging legal issues.

Learn About

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar College of Law

The University College of law was established in 1925. The College got tremendous response from the then capital city of earstwhile C.P.& Bearar Province. In the first decade the college has achieved many significant achievements . Sir. Vivian Bose was the first principal of the college. The classes of the college were conducted in the premises of 'Convocation Hall' of the University. Sir. Vivian Bose was later elevated as the Judge of High Court Nagpur. The Nagpur High Court was established in 1936. Sir. Vivian Bose was subsequently elevated to the Supreme Court.

In 1940 the college was shifted in the present premises and since then it is growing in the same premises. Initially there were part time teachers who were appointed by Nagpur University . Subsequently the full time teachers were appointed .Presently the college has the full time Principal, full time teachers and many visiting faculty members to cater the needs of the students.

We regularly organize visits to the Parliament of India for our law students. During these visits, Our law students observe the legislative process in the Parliament.

Legal Aid Programs

Our students have the chance to observe the inner workings of a police station and learn about the legal processes involved in criminal investigations and law enforcement.
